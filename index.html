Grammar (Ngữ pháp)

1. The Passive Voice (Câu bị động)

Cách dùng cơ bản: Dùng khi nhấn mạnh vào đối tượng chịu tác động, hoặc khi không biết/không quan trọng người thực hiện hành động là ai.

Công thức chung: S + be + V3/ed + (by O)
TenseActivePassive
Present SimpleThey use tablets.Tablets are used.
Past SimpleHe invented the app.The app was invented (by him).
Present ContinuousThey are upgrading the system.The system is being upgraded.
Present PerfectShe has finished the course.The course has been finished.
Modals (can, must...)We must submit it.It must be submitted.

🔥 CÁC TRƯỜNG HỢP ĐẶC BIỆT (Special Cases):

1. Động từ có 2 tân ngữ (give, send, show, buy...)
Active: They gave him a scholarship.
Passive 1 (Ưu tiên): He was given a scholarship.
Passive 2: A scholarship was given to him.
2. Cấu trúc nhờ vả (Causative Form)
Dùng khi bạn nhờ/thuê người khác làm việc gì đó cho mình.
Công thức: S + have / get + O (vật) + V3/ed + (by O người)
VD: I had my laptop repaired yesterday. (Tôi đã nhờ người sửa laptop)
3. Bị động với động từ chỉ quan điểm/ý kiến (say, think, believe, report...)
Active: People say that online learning is effective.
Passive 1: It is said that online learning is effective.
Passive 2: Online learning is said to be effective.
4. Cấu trúc Need (Cần được làm gì)
Công thức: S (vật) + need + V-ing hoặc S + need + to be V3/ed
VD: This software needs updating. = This software needs to be updated.

2. Making Suggestions (Đưa ra lời đề nghị)

Cách đưa ra lời khuyên/gợi ý:

  • Let's + V-inf: Let's use educational apps.
  • How about / What about + V-ing/Noun?: How about joining a virtual class?
  • Why don't we/you + V-inf?: Why don't you try this dictionary?
  • I suggest + V-ing: I suggest taking online quizzes.
  • I suggest (that) S + (should) + V-inf: I suggest that he should buy a tablet.
  • Đặc biệt: S + had better + V-inf (Tốt hơn hết nên làm gì): You had better update the app.
💡 Cách đáp lại lời đề nghị (Responding to suggestions):
- Đồng ý (Agree): That's a great idea! / Sounds good to me. / I couldn't agree more. / Let's do that.
- Từ chối khéo (Disagree): I'm not sure about that. / I'd rather not. / Perhaps we could do something else.
